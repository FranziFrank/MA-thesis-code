Publishing at the University of Bamberg:

FIS is the institutional repository of the University of Bamberg, where researchers can publish Open Access directly or deposit already published articles.

As a member of the University of Bamberg, you can publish with UBP or edit a journal.

Tools to Find the Right Open Access Journal

If you have a manuscript but are unsure where to publish Open Access, or about costs, oa.finder helps you find suitable OA journals or publishers for books.

DOAJ offers a comprehensive list of quality-checked Open Access journals.

B!SON is a project by TIB and SLUB Dresden, implementing a recommendation service for quality-assured Open Access journals.
