Funding

Publishing Open Access may involve publication fees. To support researchers, the University of Bamberg promotes Open Access publishing through its Open Access fund in its Open Access Declaration:

The financing of publication fees for quality-checked open access journals is supported by a central open access fund.

Further information on funding Open Access publications is available on the website of the University Library.
